Listing few career and education choices in Bharuch:

Bharuch previously known as Broach is a city at the mouth of the river Narmada in Gujarat, in western India. Bharuch is the managerial central command of Bharuch District.

Bharuch has been the home to the Gujarati Bhargav Brahmin people group for a very long time. The group follows its heredity to Maharshi Bhrigu Rishi and Bhagwan Parshuram who is viewed as manifestation of Lord Vishnu. The current day Bhargav Brahmins have moved to Mumbai, Surat, Vadodara, Ahmedabad and different nations like the US, UK and Australia.

There are various schools and universities in Bharuch which give instructions in English or Gujarati medium. Schools here are either associated to Gujarat Board, CBSE Board or the ICSE Board like Sabari Vidya Peedom, ABP School, Amity, QAC School, Holy Angels Convents, Bharatiya Vidya Bhavans, Narmada Vidyalya, DPS, and Sanskar Vidya Bhavan.

A few universities give training in different under-graduate and post-graduate streams including business and science, Narmada College of Science and Commerce, Narmada College of Management - Government Engineering College (GEC), Shroff S.R Rotary Institute of Chemical Technology etc. are known colleges since numerous years. Narmada College of Computer Application (NCCA) provides Master of Computer Application (MCA) course since 1999.

There is a designing school subsidiary to the Gujarat Technical University including Government Engineering College named as SVM Institute of Technology which offers post-graduate courses.
