Project DISHA is an initiative by L&T for rural & underprivileged schools of surat district. The project was executed by CareerNaksha team for 9th & 11th students of four schools. The entire program started with orientation about various career options in science, commerce & arts, followed by a psychometric test & its analysis for all students in groups, parental counselling and then one day soft skills workshop.
