3. Cookies

Like many web sites, this web site sets and uses cookies to enhance your user experience — to remember your personal settings, for instance. Persistent cookies remain on your computer or device after you close your browser or turn off your computer. We use cookies to see which areas and features are popular and to count visits to our web site.
